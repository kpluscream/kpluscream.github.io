Fuji Yusoki Kogyo Co., Ltd

Fuji Yusoki Kogyo Co., Ltd, founded in 1944 in Japan, devotes to development and manufacturing of robotic palletizing systems. Today, over 15,000 robots have been sold all over the world.



Palletizing Robot - EC201

New design to meet both high speed and heavy duty applications.
The award-winning EC-201 model is the top-of-the-line robot in the FUJI-ACE line and one of the highest-performance, most technologically advanced, robotic palletizer in the market today.
It is particularly well-suited for high-speed sack or beverage case palletizing lines.
