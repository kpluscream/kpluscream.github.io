Chuen Huah Chemical Co., Ltd

Chuen Huah Chemical Co., Ltd. (CHC) has been committed to corrugating industry for over 30 years. With an excellent reputation and proven experience, our machinery and chemical products have been the leading products for starch adhesive preparation around the world.With the innovative concepts created by our engineers, CHC automatic adhesive preparation machines have been designed and developed as the new standards for starch glue systems. Through integrating the no-carrier process, viscosity detection & control system, and computer control program, CHC adhesive machines provide every customer with the most convenient way to improve the efficiency of corrugating lines.We are also the main manufacturer and supplier about the waterproof material in Taiwan. Many constructor appoint our products to be its waterproof coating. In this industry, we are the leading brand cause our quality and reputation.
