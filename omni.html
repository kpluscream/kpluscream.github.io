Omni Lifter

Omni Lifter is recognized by Japan's Ministry of Land, Infrastructure, and Transport and Ministry of Health & Human Welfare as non-elevator mechanical equipment, and also as Japan's the first vertical pallet transporter.
Since the Omni Lifter is classified as non-elevator mechanical equipment, it is eligible for quarterly self-inspections only instead of costly monthly elevator inspections, which helps reduce running costs.
Inverter control smoothes acceleration and deceleration to help mitigate cargo fall worries.
Specially designed independent frame offers excellent durability and is easy to install in both new and existing facilities.
Safety features based on legal safety regulations offer operators reassurances of safety.
