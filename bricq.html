Bricq

Pioneer in the production of heavy textiles destined for industrial use, BRICQ SAS, is located near Angoulême in France. With 130 years of existence and experience, our young company began producing textiles of great use through one of its French brands, “Toison D’or”. Through the years, we have diversified industrial applications, producing textiles that come into the production processes of industries like corrugated cardboard. Today we are involved with several industrial sectors that look for resistance, porosity or one of the many features that we give the transformation of the thread in our textiles.
